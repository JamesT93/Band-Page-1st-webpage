About

Kurbside Villain are a Bristol/Bath based Alt-Rock band who formed in the summer of 2021. They consist of Barney the vocalist (no affiliation with the purple dinosaur), James Turner on guitar (not related to Alex), Wilber the drummer/Father, and Thirza (teer-za) the bassist/girl.


They started playing shows in summer 2022 so are fresh onto the South West live scene, however in this short time and they have already released an E.P and sold out a headline show. They plan to keep up the momentum in 2023 with more shows and music releases coming soon...
